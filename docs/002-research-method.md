# Research Method

Every claim must include:

- status: CONFIRMED, PROBABLE, HYPOTHESIS, or DISPROVED;
- artifact identifier and cryptographic hash;
- exact path, offset, or command used;
- observation separated from interpretation;
- reproduction steps;
- date and session identifier.

## Evidence hierarchy

1. Repeatable local observation of the supplied artifact.
2. Vendor documentation or standards documentation.
3. Independent reproduction by another researcher.
4. Specialist community reports.
5. Forum claims without evidence.

Community claims are leads, not facts.
